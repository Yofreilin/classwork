Agile Phases

Agile Phases Description
Concept a general idea, a thought, or an abstract notion formed in the mind from
specific instances or experiences
Inception the beginning or start of something, such as a project, idea, or company
Iteration the process of repeating a set of instructions or actions to achieve a desired result, often with modifications
made after each repetition
Release the act of freeing someone or something from restraint, obligation, or confinement
Maintenance the process of keeping something, like equipment or a building, in good working order through regular
care, repair, and upkeep
Retirement the act of permanently withdrawing from a working or professional career, often due to age, and living
on savings, investments, or other benefits like Social Security instead of a salary
