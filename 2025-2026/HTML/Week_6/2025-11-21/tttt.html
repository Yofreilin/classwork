Agile Phases

Agile Phases Description
Concept The team collaborates to understand the end-users' needs and the goals the software should meet. Essentially, we ask "What problem will this software solve?" and "What value will it offer to the user?"
Inception The inception phase of an Agile project refers to the early stages of planning and envisioning how the finished product will look.
Iteration This phase involves cross-functional teams who implement prioritized features from the backlog
Release During the release phase, end-users or customers receive working and potentially shippable increments of the software.
Maintenance Following the release of the software product, support and upkeep are required
Retirement In Agile, the retirement phase represents the end of the softwares life cycle.
